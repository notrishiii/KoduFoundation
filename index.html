Our Featured Projects

Project Sanjeevni

The Live Giver Our planet is moving towards destruction. We have lost many species over the years and even the future of humans are in danger. One of the biggest problems that we face is the waste production that is increasing exponentially. It has drastic effects on individual health as well as the climate that makes things worse for our future. Through this project we intend to address the issue on all three front - Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. Engaging the youth in interactive problem solving process and habituating them of a life style that is eco-friendly is one of our primary methods to ensure better future. We create channels for recycling plastic waste and promote a lifestyle that involves lesser carbon footprint. Through Project Sanjeevani we intend to create a system that brings dignity and safety to the lives of rag pickers, and a complete cycle of effective waste management.

Project Saathi

Menstruation It's not just a woman's issue. As the name suggests 'Saathi' is a gender neutral term used for friends, partner or anyone hwo supports you. Through this project we intend to take up the menstrual health and awareness conversation to the boys and men in the society. We believe the stigma and the taboo assoiated with period talks. Annot be dissolved unless we bring the other half into the conversation. We understand that not all women experience period, and not all who experience period is a woman. Spreading awareness and making the society conversation - comfortable, thereby makikg them aware and prepared to support our fellow menstruators in a healthy way is our vision.

Project Udaan

Giving Wings to Fly Rural India accounts for 65% of our country. We are known to be an agricultural nation, yet our famers are in distress due to lack of support. In Jharkhand, people living In rural areas depend on small scale farming for their daily needs and survival. They face challenges in the forms of lack of water, and unawareness of methods that ca help them increase their output with better natural means that come with advent of technology. Creating a system where we can combine the rural skills and modern techniques through sustainable methods is the intended goal of this project. Rural development through regular interventions in education and farming support can lead to holistic growth of our people and help a long way in a sustainable future for all of us.

Project Adhikaar

Our Rights Government and other organisations have created several avenues for the betterment of people from different sections of the society. Unfortunately, the benefits don't reach to the desired beneficiaries because of several hurdles in the way. During our research it was found that lack of awareness about the policies and welfare schemes meant for the people was one of the major roles why the intended result is not achieved. Through Project Adhikaar it is our attempt at bridging the gap in receiving the information and also how to avail the benefits that can help make lives a little easier for those in need.
