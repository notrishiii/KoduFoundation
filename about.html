Who are we?

The Koru Foundation is a non-profit that is working towards identifying civic problems that are a major concern for our society and use skills and technology to find solutions to those. Unscrupulous waste management is a threat to the life sustainability on planet, and developing and executing means to tackle and solve it in a sustainable way is what we are looking forward to. About a year ago, we started to create a foundation for waste management through sensitisation on waste and on the significance of its segregation by undertaking one of our key initiatives - Cleanliness Drive in the urban and rural areas of Jharkhand. This is how Greencaps came into formation, however to diversify our projects, we evolved into another non profit organization which we proudly call our Dream Venture - The Koru Foundation.

OUR VISION?

With problems, comes solutions as well. The Koru Foundation wants to be the catalyst that can speed up the recovery process and also improve it by using modern techniques and human skills, in result reducing wastes going to the landfills and increasing on the percentage of waste that can be recycled or upcycled. We envision to build a healthy ecosystem alongwith generating jobs in the rural and the marginalised sector.

Our Solution

Waste management and waste reduction

Waste management is important because improperly stored refuse can cause health, safety and economic problems. All living organisms create waste, but humans create far more waste than other species.

Sustainable agriculture and rural management

Through sustainable agriculture, GreenCaps helps communities develop forms of food production that are economically viable, ecologically sound, socially just and supportive of rural culture.

Empowering the women and children of our society

Helping the marginalised children of the society through education and health. Empowering women is essential to the health and social development of families, communities and countries.
